GOOD HEALTH AND WELLBEING

Welcome to Our Sustainable World.We are a passionate team dedicated to promoting sustainability and addressing the UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). Our mission is to raise awareness, inspire action, and contribute to a better future for our planet and its inhabitants.

Our Commitment to SDGs

At Good health and wellbeing , we are aligned with the UN Sustainable Development Goals. Our focus lies in ensuring good health and well being for all and all ages. Through our content and initiatives, we aim to make a positive impact and drive positive change in our community and beyond.

Explore our website to learn more about sustainable living practices, environmental conservation, and the steps you can take to contribute to a more sustainable world.

TARGETS AND INDICATORS

By 2030, end the epidemics of AIDS, tuberculosis, malaria and neglected tropical diseases and combat hepatitis, water-borne diseases and other communicable diseases


Indicators

  • Tuberculosis incidence per 100,000 population
  • Malaria incidence per 1,000 population

  • Number of new HIV infections per 1,000 uninfected population, by sex, age and key populations
  • Number of people requiring interventions against neglected tropical diseases
